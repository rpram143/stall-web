About Rajesh Sweet Stall

For over three decades, Rajesh Sweet Stall has been serving the finest traditional Tamil Nadu sweets. Our recipes have been carefully preserved and perfected over generations, ensuring every bite takes you back to the authentic flavors of South India.

We specialize in iconic Tamil Nadu sweets like Mysore Pak, Jangiri, Gulab Jamun, and many more, made with pure ghee, premium cashews, and traditional methods to create sweets that are not just delicious, but also made with love and care.
